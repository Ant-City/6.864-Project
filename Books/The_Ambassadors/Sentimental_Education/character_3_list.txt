

Charles Deslauriers - 
Frederic's best friend. Deslauriers is a loyal friend
to Frederic even when Frederic snubs him to pursue Madame Arnoux.
Through the ups and downs of the men's friendship, Deslauriers is
generally willing to forgive Frederic's less-than-admirable behavior. Eventually,
he tries to take some of Frederic's seeming good fortune for his
own. Deslauriers later becomes a successful clerk.
