

Sherlock Holmes - 
                A private detective and the story's protagonist. Sherlock Holmes's keen observations and ability to reason allow him to solve puzzles that stymie everyone else. Sometimes quiet and contemplative, other times bursting with energy, Holmes uses methodology that can confuse and frustrate others. He is somewhat of a mystery, rarely divulging his thoughts until he's already solved the crime.
