

mrs.[CHARACTER] - 
      mrs.[CHARACTER] is a meek but passionate woman. she and her husband hired ejlert lovborg as a tutor to their children, and mrs.[CHARACTER] grew attached to ejlert, acting as his personal secretary and aiding him in his research and writing. when ejlert leaves her estate to return to the city, mrs.[CHARACTER] comes to town and goes to tesman* for help, fearing ejlert will revert to his alcoholism. mrs.[CHARACTER] went to school with hedda and remembers being tormented by her.
