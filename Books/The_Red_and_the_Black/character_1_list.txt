


Mme. de Renal  - 
      Both an aristocrat and the wife of the town mayor, Mme. de Renal is Julien's first love interest. Mme. de Renal truly loves Julien and although she cheats on her husband, she is a symbol of moral purity and kindness. Unlike every other character, Mme. de Renal is honest, compassionate, and resourceful. She is one of the few characters in all of French literature to die of love.
