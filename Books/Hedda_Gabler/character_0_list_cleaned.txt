

[CHARACTER]  - 
      hedda is the daughter of the famous general gabler; as a child she was used to luxury and high-class living. as the play begins, she is returning from her honeymoon with jurgen tesman, a scholar with good prospects but not as much money as hedda is accustomed to. her married name is hedda tesman. hedda is an intelligent, unpredictable, and somewhat dishonest young woman who is not afraid to manipulate her husband and friends.
