

mrs.[CHARACTER]  - 
      clym[CHARACTER]'s mother, and thomasin[CHARACTER]'s aunt and guardian. a proper, class-conscious, proud woman, mrs.[CHARACTER] objects to the marriage of both her charges; as it turns out, she is entirely correct. she dies when, exhausted, she is bitten by an adder on the heath, believing that clym has utterly rejected her. the daughter of a parson, mrs.[CHARACTER] considers herself--and is considered--of a higher class than the local laborers.
