

Monsieur Morrel  - 
The kind, honest shipowner who was once Dantes's
boss. Morrel does everything in his power to free Dantes from prison
and tries to save Dantes's father from death. When Dantes emerges
from prison, he discovers that Morrel is about to descend into financial
ruin, so he carries out an elaborate plot to save his one true friend.
