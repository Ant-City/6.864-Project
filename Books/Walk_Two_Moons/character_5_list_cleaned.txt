

mrs.[CHARACTER]  - 
      phoebe's mother. like phoebe herself, mrs.[CHARACTER] is obsessed with propriety. she works diligently as a housewife and mother, baking, sewing, cooking, cleaning, chatting pleasantly at the dinner table, but becomes increasingly saddened by her family's disregard of her. convinced that her life has become insignificant and meaningless, she one day disappears in an attempt to reconcile herself to her past and her true self.
