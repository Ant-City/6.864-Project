

[CHARACTER]  - 
      the mother of martha and dickon (as well as of twelve other children), [CHARACTER] functions as a symbol for the concept of motherhood itself. she is all-nurturing, all-knowing, and appears dressed in a hooded blue cloak like that of the christian virgin mary (the mother of jesus christ). both mary and colin express the wish that she were their mother; stories of her sustain each of them before their respective transformations.
