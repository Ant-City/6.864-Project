

Mercedes - 
                    Dantes's
beautiful and good fiancee. Though Mercedes marries another man,
Fernand Mondego, while Dantes is in prison, she never stops loving
Dantes. Mercedes is one of the few whom Dantes both punishes (for
her disloyalty) and rewards (for her enduring love and underlying
goodness).
