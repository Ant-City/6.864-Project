


[CHARACTER]  - 
      tesman is an amiable, intelligent young scholar. he tries very hard to please his young wife, hedda, and often does not realize that she is manipulating him. in fact, he often seems foolish for his age, and when he annoys hedda, the audience has reason to sympathize with her. tesman is hoping for a professorship in history, and at the beginning of the play it seems that his one great rival, ejlert lovborg, a notorious alcoholic, no longer stands in tesman's way. tesman was raised by his aunt julle.
